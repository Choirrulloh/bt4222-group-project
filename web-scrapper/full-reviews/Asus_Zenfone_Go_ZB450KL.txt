The Asus ZenFone Go ZB450KL comes with a 4.5-inch TFT screen having modest resolution of 480 x 854 pixels. The device is available in various colour options which are charcoal black, pearl white, glamor red and lemon yellow. The handset weighs 135 grams and has thickness of 11.2mm.. The smartphone runs on Android v6.0 (Marshmallow) operating system with its native ZenUI 2.0 on top. The device is powered by 1.2GHz quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 410 processor, it is assisted by 1GB of RAM and Adreno 306 GPU.. The handset sports an 8MP camera on the rear having an aperture of f/2.0 with autofocus, LED flash and other features like digital zoom, auto flash, face detection, touch to focus. It comes along with 2MP camera for selfies and video calls. The device offers 8GB of internal storage which can be expanded upto 128GB using microSD card.. Coming to connectivity, the options available in this phone are dual SIM, 4G, Wi-Fi with hotspot, Bluetooth v4.0, GPS with A-GPS, Glonass and microUSB 2.0. Sensors present in this device include light, proximity, accelerometer and compass. The device is fuelled by 2,070mAh li-ion battery which is of removable nature..
