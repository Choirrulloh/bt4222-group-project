It is available in silver and gold, and will go for 899 Chinese Yuan (about PHP6,300).. Both phones are still a great catch at this price range, with a full 720p high definition display and a fingerprint scanner. Both phones will be worthy for those who like an Android phone and have a tight budget. The Redmi 3S is unlocked, making it a great option for those outside of China, and looking for a phone that can easily adopt to the local telecomm infrastructure..
